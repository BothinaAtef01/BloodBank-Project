Ways to Give

Choose your donation type

Whole Blood

The most common type of donation. It provides red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets. Crucial for trauma patients and surgery.

Time required 45-60 mins
Frequency Every 56 days
Best for All blood types

Plasma

The liquid portion of blood. Collected via an automated process that returns red cells to you. Vital for burn victims and chronic diseases.

Time required 1 hour 15 mins
Frequency Every 28 days
Best for AB positive/negative

Platelets

Tiny cells that help blood clot. Essential for cancer patients undergoing chemotherapy and those with life-threatening illnesses.

Time required 2-3 hours
Frequency Every 7 days
Best for A positive, B positive
